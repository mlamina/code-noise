**Code Noise** is a web app that allows you to generate and play various types of background noise, customizable with sliders and knobs. Whether you prefer **pink, white, or brown noise**, or even a select number of **YouTube videos for ambience**, Code Noise has got you covered! ğŸ¶

- **Noise Generation in the Browser**: No need to download anything!
